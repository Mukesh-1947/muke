What is Cybersecurity?

Cybersecurity involves protecting computer systems, networks, and data from digital attacks, theft, and damage. With the increase in cyber threats, it's essential to understand how to safeguard personal and organizational information.

Cybersecurity Best Practices

  • Use strong, unique passwords for each of your accounts.
  • Enable two-factor authentication wherever possible.
  • Keep your software and systems updated to protect against vulnerabilities.
  • Avoid clicking on suspicious links or downloading unknown attachments.
  • Regularly back up your data to recover it in case of a cyber incident.
